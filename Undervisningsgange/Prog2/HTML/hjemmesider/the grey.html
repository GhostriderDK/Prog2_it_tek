THE INTERIUS

Our symbol consists of three parts called walls. This is because when you are in the shade. The shade of grey. You are living within your own house. Three walls because it is the minimum needed to provide a full enclosure for your wellbeing. We are not materialistic and do not seek what we do not need. The walls in our symbol are parallel because our walls are still open to the world though we dwell safely within them. We are not shut off from the actions of others. We do not bury our heads in the sand and pretend all is right. We simply except the world as it unfolds.

Finally, the center. The Self. You are your own house within the walls of interius. Within your soul is the true source of our shade yet still empty and open. Not filled with life's inconveniences or cares.

While symbolic of our way life. The interius is also representative of the path to the shade of grey. The walls of interius are broken into three lessons to become a member in our chain.

LATUS
Our outer perception of the world and the way we are perceived by others.

CENTRUM
The way we live as ourselves for ourselves.

INANIS
Balance. To be truly grey.
